Grammar Guide

The picture is above the sofa.above ↔ under
The shower is next to the tub.next to = beside
The stove is between the table and the bookcase.between = in the middle of two things
The table is in front of the bookcase.in front of ↔ behind
Use above, under, next to, between, behind, and in front of to say where things are.
